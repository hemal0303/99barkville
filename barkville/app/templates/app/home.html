About Us

Welcome to BarkVille Foundation

Barkville Foundation is situated on 17,000 sqft land in Dabhoda, Gandhinagar, India. and has been dedicated to second chances. We provide a safe and caring home to all animals in need – large and small – that have been abused, neglected or abandoned. Our mission is to end the cycle of abuse and pet overpopulation through our rescue and investigation efforts, spay/neuter programs and educational classes. We are committed to creating lasting relationships between people and animals through our adoption programs
